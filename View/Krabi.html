Phi Phi Islands

A stunning tropical paradise in Krabi

Overview

The Phi Phi Islands are one of the most popular tourist destinations in Thailand. Located in Krabi Province, this group of islands offers stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and an array of water activities.

Highlights

  • Relax on pristine beaches like Maya Bay.
  • Snorkel or dive to explore vibrant coral reefs.
  • Enjoy scenic boat tours around the islands.

How to Get There

Phi Phi Islands can be reached by ferry or speedboat from Phuket or Krabi. The journey typically takes 1-2 hours depending on the departure point.
