Bangkok

The vibrant capital city of Thailand

Overview

Bangkok, Thailand's bustling capital, is known for its vibrant street life, grand palaces, and cultural landmarks such as Wat Arun and the Grand Palace.

Highlights

  • Visit the iconic Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew.
  • Shop at Chatuchak Weekend Market.
  • Enjoy street food along Yaowarat Road (Chinatown).

How to Get There

Bangkok is well-connected by flights, buses, and trains. The main airport is Suvarnabhumi International Airport (BKK).
