Zhangjiajie

A stunning national forest park in China

Overview

Zhangjiajie is famous for its towering sandstone pillars, lush forests, and the inspiration for the movie Avatar.

Highlights

  • Visit the Avatar Hallelujah Mountain.
  • Walk across the Zhangjiajie Glass Bridge.
  • Enjoy breathtaking views from the Tianmen Mountain Cableway.
