Chiang Mai

Cultural heart of Northern Thailand

Overview

Chiang Mai is a city rich in culture, temples, and beautiful natural landscapes. It's the gateway to Northern Thailand's mountains and forests.

Highlights

  • Explore Doi Suthep Temple overlooking the city.
  • Visit local night markets for crafts and food.
  • Take an elephant sanctuary tour.

How to Get There

Chiang Mai has an international airport (CNX) with direct flights from Bangkok and other cities.
